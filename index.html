About me

My passions are people, problem solving, and enjoying the fruits of my labor. I have been with Ford Motor Credit for 5 years and I have been on the quality assurance team for 2 years. I have enjoyed my time as a Quality Assurance Coach because it gives me the opportunity to directly help the team members I evaluate develope their skills and be more impactful with the organization. Before I joined Ford Motor Credit I worked as a Property Manager who over saw an apartment community that housed over 100 apartment homes.

I want to become a software engineer because I want to empower myself to change the world and to challange myself with a skill set that I have very little understanding of. While most of my creative hobbies result in physical results I am very excited about the creative possibilities coding will provide me because there are no physical limitations which means virtually anything is possible! I also look forward to collaborating with different people on various projects because there is always something new to learn and I believe learning to code will give me the opportunity to learn from and collaborate with people that have different backgrounds and experiences.
